Vitae

Dr. Thanana has been a visiting scholar, starting from March 2018. Prior to this, he held a postdoctoral fellow for two years. He achieved his M.Eng. degree and Ph.D. degree with excellent thesis and outstanding thesis, respectively.

Research Philosophy in Control

His research concerns with seeking a control tool applying to a discipline in engineering science (i.e., bionic, robotics, power, autonomous systems) with understanding the underlying physical phenomena, where the foundations of systems \(\&\) control enable improved performance and robustness realized through model-free control and\(/\)or model-based control.
