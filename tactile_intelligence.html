Tactile Intelligence Loop

Uncertainty-aware tactile perception + Bayesian/Deep MPC for dexterous manipulation

Modern robots struggle with contact-rich tasks because friction, slip, and occlusion create uncertainty at the interface.

Tactile Intelligence Loop is my research vision: closing the loop from tactile sensing → latent stochastic modeling → uncertainty-aware control.
