Tactile Intelligence Loop

Uncertainty-aware tactile perception + Bayesian/Deep MPC for dexterous manipulation

Modern robots struggle with contact-rich tasks because friction, slip, and occlusion create uncertainty at the interface. Tactile Intelligence Loop is my research vision: closing the loop from tactile sensing → latent stochastic modeling → uncertainty-aware control.

Core Ideas

  • Latent tactile state: deep stochastic state-space models capturing uncertainty and temporal correlation.
  • Policy optimization: reinforcement learning for long-term adaptation, MPC for constraint satisfaction.
  • Bidirectional touch: surface haptics to actively modulate fingertip friction as both sensing and communication.

Objective

Develop robots that “yield to uncertainty” while maintaining stability, safety, and dexterity—advancing tactile robotics for human-compatible manipulation.
